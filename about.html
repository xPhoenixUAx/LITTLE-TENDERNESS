Frequently Asked Questions

Your digital journey clarified

Explore the questions clients usually ask before starting structured marketing work with A LITTLE TENDERNESS s.r.o. Clear expectations make the first conversation faster, sharper, and more useful.

How does pricing work for your services?

Pricing depends on the scope, channels, campaign volume, and level of strategic support required. We usually begin with a short discovery conversation, then recommend a clear starting package or a custom monthly engagement.

Can we customize a plan around our specific requirements?

Yes. Most projects are shaped around the current business stage, available internal resources, market priorities, and what needs to be improved first: positioning, acquisition, landing pages, reporting, or creative output.

What results can we expect from digital marketing support?

We do not promise fixed outcomes, because performance depends on market conditions, budgets, offers, and conversion readiness. Our role is to improve campaign structure, clarity, measurement, and the quality of decisions over time.

How long does it take to see meaningful progress?

Foundational improvements can often be identified quickly. Campaign learning, conversion improvements, and more reliable acquisition patterns usually need several weeks of testing, reporting, and optimization.

Do you offer ongoing support and optimization?

Yes. We can support campaign reviews, testing priorities, reporting rhythm, creative direction, landing-page recommendations, and ongoing optimization so marketing work stays structured after launch.
