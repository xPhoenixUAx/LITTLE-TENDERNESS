Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 January 2026 · Last updated: 1 January 2026
Website: littletendernessads.comEmail: support@littletendernessads.com

This Privacy Policy explains how A LITTLE TENDERNESS s.r.o. ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use littletendernessads.com (the "Website").

01. Who We Are

This Website is operated by A LITTLE TENDERNESS s.r.o..

We provide digital marketing, advertising, and campaign execution services, supporting businesses with customer acquisition, creative campaigns, and performance-driven digital growth across Europe and international markets.

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR"), A LITTLE TENDERNESS s.r.o. acts as the data controller in relation to personal data collected through this Website.

We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our website visitors, prospective clients, and business partners.

02. Data We Collect

We may collect personal data when you interact with our Website, submit inquiries, or engage with our services.

Categories of data include:

  • Identity data - name, surname, company name, job title
  • Contact data - email address, phone number, business address
  • Financial data - billing information, VAT numbers, invoicing details where applicable
  • Technical data - IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, time zone
  • Usage data - pages visited, session duration, navigation paths, on-site interactions
  • Marketing data - communication preferences and campaign engagement
  • Business data - information related to services such as marketing goals, budgets, and project scope

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data. If such data is provided, it will be handled appropriately or deleted where necessary.

03. How We Use Your Data

  • respond to inquiries and requests
  • provide, manage, and improve our services
  • maintain business relationships
  • process payments and invoices
  • analyse website performance and user behaviour
  • support advertising and marketing activities
  • protect the Website and prevent misuse
  • comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for unrelated or incompatible purposes.

04. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance - to deliver requested services
  • Legitimate interests - to operate and improve our business and Website
  • Legal obligations - to comply with applicable regulations
  • Consent - for marketing communications and cookies where required

You may withdraw consent at any time.

05. Data Retention

  • client and financial data - up to 7 years
  • inquiry data - up to 2 years
  • analytics data - up to 26 months
  • marketing data - until consent withdrawal

Where appropriate, data may be anonymised for analytical use.

06. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell personal data.

We may share data with:

  • service providers such as hosting, CRM, and communication tools
  • advertising platforms such as Google and Meta
  • analytics providers
  • professional advisers
  • public authorities where required by law

All third parties are required to process data securely and lawfully.

07. International Data Transfers

Some service providers may process data outside the EEA. In such cases, we implement appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses and recognised data protection mechanisms.

08. Your Rights

You have the right to access your personal data, request correction, request deletion, restrict processing, receive your data, object to processing, and withdraw consent. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a relevant supervisory authority.

09. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to ensure proper website functionality, analyse usage, and support advertising and remarketing. For more details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.
